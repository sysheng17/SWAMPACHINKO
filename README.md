# SWAMPACHINKO

Swampachinko 是一款以沼澤柏青哥為主題的惡搞模擬遊戲。 <br> 

揹負 10 億債務的你，必須在沼澤機台上放手一搏。 <br> 
操控發射器將鋼珠打入得分區，賺取金幣償還債務。 <br> 

▸ 挖掘模式：深入地下隧道採集金幣，小心疲勞累積  <br> 
▸ 三種機台：從入門的銅機台到高風險的金機台  <br> 
▸ 獨特的疲勞系統與自動球貸機制  <br> 
▸ 支援 6 種語言：日本語、繁體中文、簡體中文、English、한국어、Español <br> 

本遊戲為純粹之戲仿創作，與現實賭博行為無關。

---

Swampachinko is a parody pachinko simulation game set in a swamp.

Buried under 1 billion in debt, you must take your chances at the swamp machines.  <br> 
Launch steel balls into the scoring zones to earn coins and repay your debt. <br> 

▸ Dig mode: mine coins in underground tunnels, watch your fatigue  <br> 
▸ Three machine tiers: from entry-level Copper to high-risk Gold  <br> 
▸ Unique fatigue system and auto ball loan mechanics  <br> 
▸ Supports 6 languages: 日本語, 繁體中文, 简体中文, English, 한국어, Español <br> 

This game is a work of parody and has no association with real-world gambling.
