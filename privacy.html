Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Information Collection and Processing

Swampachinko (hereinafter referred to as "the Application") is a fully offline single-player game. The developer does not actively collect, store, transmit, or otherwise process any personally identifiable information of users.

2. Data Storage

Game progress and related data (including but not limited to in-game currency, debt amounts, and level progress) are stored exclusively in the local internal storage of the user's device. Such data is not accessed, backed up, or transmitted by the developer or any third party via servers.

3. Third-Party Services

This Application does not integrate any third-party advertising services, user behavior tracking tools, or analytics software development kits (e.g., Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, etc.). Accordingly, no data is transmitted to any third-party servers.

4. Device Permissions

This Application does not require any special device permissions. Touch input necessary for gameplay is handled by the operating system's standard mechanisms and does not require additional authorization.
